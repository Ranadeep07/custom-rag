Company Knowledge Base
======================

Refund Policy
-------------
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase if the product has not been heavily used. 
Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.
Refund requests made after 14 days may be approved only in exceptional cases.

Subscription cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
No partial refunds are provided for unused subscription time.

---

Shipping Information
--------------------
We offer standard and express shipping options.
Standard shipping typically takes 5 to 7 business days.
Express shipping typically takes 1 to 2 business days.

Shipping times may vary depending on the destination country and customs processing.
Tracking information is sent via email once the order has shipped.

---

Account Management
------------------
Users can update their email address, password, and profile information from the Account Settings page.
If a user forgets their password, they can reset it using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

Accounts that remain inactive for more than 24 months may be automatically deactivated.
Deactivated accounts can be reactivated by contacting customer support.

---

Data Privacy
------------
We collect only the minimum amount of personal data required to provide our services.
Personal data is never sold to third parties.

User data is stored in encrypted databases and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
Users may request deletion of their personal data by submitting a data removal request.

---

Technical Support
-----------------
Technical support is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM (UTC).
Support requests can be submitted through the Help Center or by email.

Critical system outages are prioritized and typically responded to within 1 hour.
Non-critical issues are responded to within 24 business hours.

---

Security Practices
------------------
All user passwords are hashed using industry-standard hashing algorithms.
Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available and strongly recommended.

We regularly conduct security audits and penetration testing to identify vulnerabilities.
